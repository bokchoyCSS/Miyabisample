Our Story

Rooted in Japanese tradition and attuned to modern life.

We cherish the beauty that lives in quiet moments.

From farm to cup

A connection across continents

Our tea is more than a drink — it’s a moment to restore the mind and spirit. In the rush of everyday life, we offer a quiet pause, a gentle breath.

Our hope is to create meaningful connections between Japanese tea artisans and Europe: honouring the people and the place behind every harvest, and carrying their craft to a new table here in Amsterdam.
